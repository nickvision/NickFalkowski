Grasping the Ouroboros


I’ll take the kiss of the current flares to
Hold my light from escaping

Too swift before the drums despite
Youth's myths ever fading with

The oompah band of this continuous absurdity.
I’ll make these decisions righteous as

The ever righteous; God my only
Barometer in these inclement times.

Certain in this uncertainty I strive again to strike
Apart the very foundations of my doubt, for

To be content with this discontent may yet
Herald the dawning of a certain kind

Of peace, or at least a masquerade at light
Pretty enough that it may in turn fool enough

For long enough to make it surely enough
That the whole thing might feel somewhat

Bearable. Yet still I feel like Barabus spared
Only to inherit a millennia of condemnation:

Speared forever upon a crescendo of deepest longing.
The present presents little respite or salve here for

The cuts the future currents carve through
My every thought of what it will take to

Continue to move so as to still breathe without
Giving my true position away - timid mouse

In a hovel of words, I preserve myself by
These sickly diatribes against the flagrant

Countenance with which this universe has chosen
To present itself to me; knowing that in saying that

I can’t escape the horrid shape my craven
Craving has long carved it into - guilty at

The foot of the cross I know I’m surely
Lost, destined to carry on so, long and along,

Belligerent and begrudging, until I
Die; and in forgetting am forgotten.

Personal Investigator


I take note of the one taking notes
Outside the back of the bushes, smiling

Innocuously? That grin borders
On incoherence. I’ve inherited more

Anxiety than you could know; unless
You know, of course, and so we must

Dance always far enough apart so as to
Trip each other only minorly, the great

Artistic collapse having little to speak for itself
Besides its own infallible fury that furnishes

The room within which one can feel suitably
Tortured. But I’m not the one here raising

Torches - this monster has become as kin
To me, a subject so akin to those writings

That ramble down the rabbit holes of purposeful
Purposelessness. Another great thrust follows

Into a space whose only response is to call absently
For more; and more; with no reward returned

To the resolve that first took the plunge, except
More depths, more darkness, and the maddening

Momentum to somehow carry itself on, striking
Desperate matches only to be

Lost inconsolably within moments of taking
Light, absorbed by the whole of the hole this

Searching has irrevocably torn through
The pretty patchwork of the world we

Slept so softly underneath, obliviously
Dreaming we knew meaning, could

Account for all the hardships and
The headaches, knowing intuitively

The intricacy with which this world is fashioned;
The celestial watch ticking to a time which

We felt we could cohere, if only we might but
Pierce the firmament that has kept us held

Safe below the high heavens, gazing ever
Upwards, watched down upon by some

Unique intelligence, not unlike our own, yet
So unlike our own as to make all of this

Impossibility somehow possible - filling us
With apostolistic intent to spread a message

To those so frantically grasping that they now
Could relax their grip. But that artifice will

Not outlive ourselves. Rather than a clock
We should have built a lock with which

To hold down and back all our wondering
For it shall always eventually extend far beyond

The extent of our knowledge, wandering
Into that place beyond time and space

Where meaning hangs upon a rope tugged
Heavier the harder you struggle now to

Wriggle free - for beyond that promise is a fear
Far greater than that of death - that there is

In fact behind it all nothing quite
Behind it all, except this person

Barely known to us, fully one
With us, sitting back, smiling,

Taking down notes on our movements
As we drift onwards through the void.
